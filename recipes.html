Homemade Whipped Cream

  • 2 Cups - Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 1 Cup - Powdered Sugar
  • 2 tsp - Vanilla Extract
  • 1/8 tsp - Almond Extract

Directions

  1. 1. Use an electric mixer to combine ingredients. First add heavy cream and mix at low speed for 1 minute.
  2. 2. Once the cream thickens, add powdered sugar and vanilla extract and mix slowly to not cause a dust storm.
  3. 3. Once well combined, increase to high speed until stiff peaks are formed. Keep in the fridge or use right away.

Cream Cheese Whipped Cream

  • 8oz - Cream Cheese
  • 2 cups - Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 tsp - Vanilla Extract
  • 1 cup - Granulated Sugar

    • Directions

  • 1. Allow cream cheese to thaw at room temperature, about an hour. Don't go more than an hour or else whipped cream will be soupy.
  • 2. Add cream cheese and sugar to misxing bowl and mix on low for 1 minute.
  • 3. Add heavy cream and mix on low for 2 minutes
  • 4. Raise mixing speed to high until stiff peaks form.
